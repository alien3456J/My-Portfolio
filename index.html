About Me

Hello! I am a 3D modeler and developer interested in creating passion projects and serious games.

Experience

I've been building games for several years — mainly on Roblox and Minecraft — and honestly it started as just messing around and turned into something real.

On Roblox, I've worked across multiple projects that have collectively pulled in over 52 million Robux (~$197,595 USD). That kind of scale taught me a lot fast — how to keep players coming back, how to monetize without ruining the experience, and how to build systems that actually hold up under pressure.

On the Minecraft side I've done everything from custom plugins to full server setups. It's been consistently generating a few thousand dollars a month, which has given me a solid feel for managing live communities and shipping content on a real schedule.

At the end of the day I just like making things that feel good to play. Whether that's scripting a mechanic, tuning a system, or fixing something that's been bugging me — I care about the details and I work well with others who do too.
